International Research Olympiad

The International Research Olympiad (IRO) is dedicated to honing analytical and critical thinking skills for student researchers—essential in scientific exploration. The IRO fosters a culture of rigorous research and skill-building, not just memorizing formulae. The practical applications of knowledge and big-picture thinking are tested in the 2024 IRO examinations, allowing students to walk away with a deeper passion for scientific inquiry. Serving as our Partners is a big boost for both sides into creating the highly competitive environment.

Our Mission

At Endeavor, our mission is to empower every student with the comprehensive experience of being a researcher, seamlessly integrating this into their STEM education to foster curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds can explore the world of research, develop their skills, and contribute to solving real-world challenges.

By offering both a supportive research community and advanced programs, we aim to nurture the next generation of scientists, engineers, and thinkers who are not only equipped with knowledge but are also inspired to make a meaningful impact on society. Through Endeavor, students gain the tools and guidance they need to excel academically, gain admission to top colleges, and continue their journey of discovery and contribution to the betterment of the world.
